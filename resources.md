Prism Escape (Endless Survival Game)

What it is:
Player square dodges falling / moving hazards that get faster over time.

instant movement = instant dopamine
difficulty scaling feels “smart”
easy to extend live

Core mechanics (simple):
keyboard input
collision detection
score over time
increasing spawn rate / speed

Upgrades:
screen shake on hit
slow-motion on near miss
color shift as difficulty increases
particle explosion on death
